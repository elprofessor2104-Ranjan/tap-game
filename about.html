About EL Rewards

EL Rewards is a simple and transparent platform that lets users earn small rewards by performing lightweight, entry-level tasks using their device. Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use experience so anyone — students, homemakers, or working professionals — can earn modest supplementary income in their free time.

What we offer

  • Easy activities like tapping, short tasks, and passive engagement that earn points.
  • A clear conversion rate and redemption policy so users know how points convert to money.
  • Local storage of point counts on the user's device — we do not require sign-in to accrue points.

Our principles

  • Transparency: Conversion rates, minimum thresholds, and payout rules are clearly stated.
  • Privacy: We only store the minimum data required to operate the app; details are in our Privacy Policy.
  • Fairness: We aim to provide a reliable reward experience and prevent abuse of the system.

How to get started

Open the Home page, tap the circular “Tap Here” button to collect points, and watch your balance increase. When you reach the minimum threshold, you may request a redemption via UPI (details in Terms & Conditions).

If you have questions, see our Contact page for support details.
